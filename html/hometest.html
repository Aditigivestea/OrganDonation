Inspiring Stories

Real people, real lives transformed through organ donation

Preeti Unhale

Heart Recipient

Mrs. Preeti Unhale, once an active college student, was diagnosed with cardiomyopathy in 1993, and her condition worsened over the years. In 2001, she received a life-saving heart transplant thanks to a donor family who chose to donate their 14-year-old son's heart after a tragic accident. Today, she leads a normal, fulfilling life, emphasizing the profound impact and importance of organ donation.

Rolly Prajapati

Donor saved lives of multiple

Rolly Prajapati, a six-year-old girl from Noida, was tragically killed by a stray bullet and later declared brain dead. Her parents made the rare decision to donate her organs, saving multiple lives and making her the youngest donor at AIIMS. Her story is now seen as a potential turning point for organ donation awareness in India. Rolly parents beared the loss ,still took a life changing decision for others.
